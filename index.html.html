Official Driving Guide

Learn Before You Drive

Official guidelines as per Ministry of Road Transport & Highways (MoRTH), Government of India.

🇮🇳
MoRTH Certified GuidelinesGovernment of India | Indian Roads Congress (IRC)
🛑

Red — Stop

Halt completely before the stop line. Crossing a red light attracts heavy penalties.

⚠️

Amber — Slow Down

Slow down and be alert. Do not speed through. Prepare to stop.

✅

Green — Go

Proceed if road ahead is clear. Check for pedestrians before moving.

➡️

Green Arrow

Move only in the arrow direction, even if the main signal is red.

🔆

Flashing Amber

Proceed with caution. A warning signal — not a full stop.

🚶

Pedestrian Walk

Vehicles must yield completely to pedestrians at marked crossings.

🔴 Mandatory Signs — Must Follow (Red Bordered Circles)

🛑

Stop

Come to a complete halt. No exceptions.

⛔

No Entry

Entry into this road is strictly prohibited.

🔃

Give Way

Yield to traffic coming from your right.

🚫

No Overtaking

Overtaking not permitted on this stretch.

↩️

No U-Turn

U-turns are prohibited here.

🔇

Horn Prohibited

Silence zones — near hospitals and schools.

⚠️ Cautionary Signs — Warnings (Red Bordered Triangles)

↪️

Sharp Turn Ahead

Slow down — sudden curve ahead.

🌉

Narrow Bridge

Reduce speed at this crossing.

🚸

Pedestrian Crossing

Watch for people crossing ahead.

🏫

School Ahead

Drive slowly in school zones.

🔨

Men at Work

Construction zone — be cautious.

🛣️

Road Narrows

The road ahead becomes narrower.

📄

Carry Documents

Always carry Driving Licence, RC Book, Insurance and Pollution Certificate.

🪑

Seat Belts Mandatory

Both driver and front-seat passenger must wear seat belts at all times.

📵

No Mobile Phones

Never use a mobile phone while the vehicle is in motion.

➡️

Overtake from Right

Always overtake only from the right side of the vehicle ahead.

🚑

Emergency Vehicles

Always give way to ambulances, fire engines and police vehicles.

🍺

No Drunk Driving

Never drive under influence of alcohol or drugs. Heavy penalties apply.

🛣️

Lane Discipline

Do not switch lanes without indicators. Stay in your lane.

🔞

Minimum Age 18

Minimum legal age to drive a Light Motor Vehicle in India is 18 years.

ℹ️ 2024 MoRTH Rule: Accredited driving schools can now issue eligibility certificates — reducing the need for a separate RTO driving test. morth.nic.in →
🤚

Slowing Down

Extend right arm out of window and swing it gently up and down.

✋

Stopping

Raise the right arm vertically to signal you are stopping.

👉

Turning Right

Extend right arm straight out of window, palm facing forward.

🔄

Turning Left

Extend right arm and rotate hand in an anti-clockwise direction.

💡 Use vehicle indicators wherever possible. Hand signals are used when indicators are non-functional or not clearly visible to others.
